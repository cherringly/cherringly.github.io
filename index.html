About Me

I am studying electrical engineering and neuroscience, with a neuro concentration in computational neuroscience. My primary research interest is brain-computer interfaces. I hope to work as a BCI researcher to expand what is possible. Things like enhancing communication for tetraplegic patients, generating feeling in artificial limbs, recording/sharing memories to perhaps one day achieve an unprecedented level of global empathy.

A quote I really like from Max Hodak (one of the founding members of Neuralink): “I believe that one of the essential goals of our generation is to understand the physics of consciousness and learn to engineer experience directly.”
